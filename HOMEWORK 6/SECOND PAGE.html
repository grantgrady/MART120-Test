About Maison Margiela

Maison Margiela, founded by the visionary designer Martin Margiela, has always been known for its high class approach to fashion. In one of their most iconic moments, the brand introduced the concept of "deconstruction," presenting fashion in a way no one had ever seen before. Their famous Tabi boots, with split-toe designs, are importrant pices of the brand's philosophy of combining fashion and art.

Some Iconic Margiela Pieces

The Tabi boot, first presented in 1989, challenged traditional shoe design and became an enduring symbol of the Maison.
